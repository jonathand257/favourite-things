Favourite holiday destinations

  3. Bergamo - Italy
  4. Istanbul - Turkey
  5. Stone Town - Zanzibar

My favourite city in the UK is Manchester - Here are my top 5 places to visit

  1. Old Trafford - Home of Manchester United
  2. Manchester Town Hall - Stunning Neo-gothic building
  3. Manchester Museum of Science and Industry - home of the industrial revolution
  4. National Football Museum - The worlds biggest and best football museum
